## GTVL is best known for our quality and speed-to-market techniques. As of now we deal with following departments:

**Management Operations:** 
-	Manage SKUs for GTVL
-	Manage customers/stores for GTVL. 
-	Manage supervisors for GTVL and Handles allocation of one supervisor to each customers/stores. 
-	Manage promodizers for GTVLand Handles allocation of one or multiple promodizers to each customer/store.
**Promodizer Operations**
-	Records sales for SKUs using barcode scanning (scan multiple barcodes in a single go and submit) for one of the assigned customers/scores
-	Records their daily attendance
**Supervisor Operations**
-	Records sales for SKUs using barcode scanning (scan multiple barcodes in a single go and submit) for one of the assigned customers/scores
-	Records their daily attendance
-       Manages Promodizers allocated to them and can add new promodizers as well to one of their assigned stores
**Sales Operations**
-	Tracking and visualizing Sales recorded by Promodizers and Supervisors
